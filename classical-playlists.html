Classical Playlists

Curated collections for every mood and moment

Why listen to this kind of music?
  • I enjoy all kinds of music (jazz, rap, drum and bass, garage, etc) but classical music is the most full of 'music'. Every song has something nice going on.
  • It's such a broad genre. The difference between Bach (1600's) and Rachmaninoff (1900's) is as big as the difference between smooth jazz and heavy metal.
  • The bigger, longer pieces feel like watching a film. They create an atmosphere, build up tension in different ways, and then everything resolves nicely in the end.
  • These songs don't get old like pop songs do. On a first listen, they might just come across as 'nice', but after each listen, you'll hear more layers of music, and eventually you'll be wanting to sing along to the chorus.
  • On my commute to work, on the underground, I don't feel stressed like I used to. Now I'm just vibing and having a good time. This music is great food for the soul.
  • I hope you enjoy these playlists as much as I do!
